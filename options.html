Intervention Levels

Scroll Limiter uses progressive interventions as you continue scrolling:

1 Level 1: Slow Down

When you exceed the threshold, scrolling will be slowed down to encourage mindful browsing.

Activates at (Threshold × Multiplier) pixels of scrolling.

2 Level 2: Visual Effects

If you continue scrolling, visual effects like screen vibration and dimming will be applied.

Activates at (Threshold × Multiplier) pixels of scrolling.

3 Level 3: Block Scrolling

As a final intervention, scrolling will be blocked with a message encouraging you to take a break.

Activates at (Threshold × Multiplier) pixels of scrolling.
